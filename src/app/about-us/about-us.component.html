About Us

Non-Profit Youth Organization and AAU Program based in Southern NH "We don't sell kids dreams, we prove to them that ANYTHING is possible" #DCBD #WeAreOne

The DC Blue Devils - NH is a non-profit AAU basketball and educational program based in Manchester, NH (the greater Boston area). The DC Blue Devils - NH was founded by TJ Thompson, a professional basketball player, competing in the premier leagues in Europe. DCBD is sponsored by Demarcus Cousins of the Sacramento Kings who is another professional athlete who believes in giving back to the community and empowering the youth as much as TJ does. We believe in encouraging our youth to become leaders on and off the court. DC Blue Devils - NH is based on the idea that we can use basketball to encourage kids to make their educational and personal values a priority. The players selected to partake in this elite program must adhere to strict educational and personal conduct guidelines in order to be eligible to participate in the AAU element. Our top priority is helping our players get to college whether it is for academics, basketball or both. The youth are our future and TJ and Demarcus are proud to be part of a program that provides tomorrow's leaders with a safe and enriching environment encouraging education, determination, hard work and appreciation and acceptance of all.
